Things I've read lately

Last Updated: 03/31/2019

Book Comments Review
Digital Minimalism: Choosing a Focused Life in a Noisy World Okay. The core premise--that digital products have taken away our agency + made us addictied--was strong, but the book didn't say much more than that. Everything else (e.g. tips, anecdotes, etc.) felt like verbose fluff. 3/5
To Engineer Is Human: The Role of Failure in Successful Design I liked the book's glorifiaction of engineering as a human pursuit. I also appreciated the various stories--some were inspiring. That said, the book dragged on too long. 3.5/5
Genghis Khan and the Making of the Modern World Really interesting book. There wasn't a lot of information about Genghis Khan to actually say, but the context surrounding him and the little we do know was great. 4.5/5
